Healing Safely

Professional Aftercare

Tattoo Instructions

Do's

  • Keep it clean: Wash gently with mild soap after 2-4 hours.
  • Moisturize: Apply a paper-thin layer of ointment twice a day.
  • Let it flake: It is normal to peel. Let the skin flake off naturally.

Don'ts

  • Don't Scratch: Scratching pulls out pigment.
  • No Swimming: Avoid pools and lakes for 3 weeks.
  • Avoid Sun: Keep the healing skin out of direct sunlight.

Piercing Instructions

Do's

  • Saline Rinses: Clean twice daily with sterile saline solution.
  • Dry thoroughly: Gently pat dry with clean paper towels.
  • Leave jewelry in: Keep the starter post in for full healing.

Don'ts

  • Don't Twist: Do not rotate jewelry while dry.
  • No Chemicals: Avoid alcohol or hydrogen peroxide.
  • Avoid Pressure: Do not apply body weight or sleep on it.
